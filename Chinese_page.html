Peking Duck

Recipe for Peking Duck

    Ingredients

  • 1 duck approx. 2.4kg in weight. Innards removed.
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
    • Spicy plum Sauce:
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 red onion peeled and chopped
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger peeled and chopped finely
  • 4 cloves garlic peeled and chopped/crushed
  • 600 g ripe plums de-stoned and cut into small (1 cm pieces)
  • 6 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp Chinese rice vinegar
  • 4 tbsp Sriracha
